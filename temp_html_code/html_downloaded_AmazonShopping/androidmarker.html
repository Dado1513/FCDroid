Instructions for printing and using the Amazon Marker

How does this work?

Look at the Marker through your phone to get a view of the room with the TV in it.

Step 1 - Print the Marker

Print the Marker that we will email to you, on 8.5 x 11 (Letter size) paper.

Step 2 - Hang the Marker

Place the Marker where you would like to view your new TV.

Tip: We recommend taping the Marker with transparent tape on all four corners like this:

Step 3 - Point your phone at the Marker

Go back to the Amazon app and point your phone at the Marker to see how the TV looks in your room.
