Instructions for using
US dollar bills

How does this work?

Look at the dollar bill through your phone to get a view of the room with the TV in it.

Step 1 - Get a US dollar bill

You can use any denomination US dollar bill.

Tip: For best results, we recommend using a crisp, clean bill.

Step 2 - Hang the bill

Place the dollar bill where you would like to view your new TV.

Step 3 - Point your phone at the dollar bill

Go back to the Amazon app and point your phone at the US dollar bill to see how the TV looks in your room.
